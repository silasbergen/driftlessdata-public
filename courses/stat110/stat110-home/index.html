Midterm exam

The midterm exam will be available in D2L beginning Friday, May 25 and must be complete by Monday, June 4 by 11:59 pm. The exam is in two parts, both in D2L: a multiple choice portion (which must completed first) and a short-answer portion (which will be available to you only after completeing the multiple choice portion). You will have 50 minutes on each portion. You may use your notes on the exam; however both portions will require the use of Lockdown Browser.

Study up for the midterm exam by taking the practice midterm exam below and checking your answers with the key. The midterm exam will cover material from Lectures 1-10.
