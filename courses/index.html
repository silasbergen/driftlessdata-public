Syllabus

STAT 460: Mathematical statistics II Spring 2019 Prerequisite: Stat 450 Course summary STAT 460 is a continuation of the mathematical statistics sequence. Stat 460 turns from properties of a single distribution (the focus of Stat 450), and turns to properties of estimation and foundational concepts of statistical inference. Specific topics include consistency, bias, and methods of finding estimators; confidence interval coverage; hypothesis test properties and optimum R will be of even greater use to us in this semester.
