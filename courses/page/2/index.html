Syllabus (Fall 2017)

Data summarization and visualization (Fall 2017) Course overview The amount and extent of data in our world is growing extremely rapidly. As such there is an increasing need for analysts skilled in summarizing data. Visualizations are an immensely popular way of summarizing data, as they allow viewers to efficiently translate data into information. Modern visualizations are either static or interactive, with a growing trend towards the latter. In this course we will discuss how we perceive information visually, how to identify and avoid data visualization pitfalls, and how to create effective static and interactive visualizations.
